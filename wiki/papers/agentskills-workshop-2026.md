# Agent Skills '26 (ACM CAIS Workshop)

**When/Where:** May 26, 2026 · San Jose, CA · Full day  
**Scope:** First workshop dedicated to design, evaluation, and optimization of Agent Skills / procedural knowledge for LLM agents

## Scale

- 103 submissions → 45 posters + 6 orals (non-archival)
- OpenReview: ACM.org/CAIS/2026/Workshop/AgentSkills

## Field statistics highlighted

- 8 focused Skills papers Feb–Mar 2026
- 3,984-skill audit: **37% security flaws**
- SkillsBench: curated skills **+16.2 pass-rate points**; self-generated skills hurt ~1/3 of tasks

## Lifecycle topics (maps to our catalog)

1. Applications & composition
2. Evaluation & benchmarking (Skills as first-class artifacts)
3. Safety & supply chain (formal verification, malicious skills)
4. Improvement & learning (generation, RL, trajectory mining)

## Why it matters

Academic institutionalization of the skill-bundle research object. SkillsBench 1.0 launch party (DeepMind/BenchFlow/Kaggle) the next day signals evaluation becoming productized. Sponsors include K-Dense (see [[scientific-agent-skills-kdense]]).
