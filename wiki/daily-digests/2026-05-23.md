# Daily Digest — 2026-05-23 (Iteration 5)

## Key Observations After 5 Iterations
- Rules and governance layers are more mature than expected
- Ontology integration is the second strongest area
- SHACL and SSSOM remain very difficult to find in agent skill contexts
